# Responsible Disclosure Policy

We take the security of our users and systems seriously. We appreciate the security community's efforts to help us improve the Windows Penetration Toolkit.

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a vulnerability, we ask that you:

1.  **Do not** exploit the vulnerability beyond what is necessary to demonstrate it.
2.  **Do not** access, modify, or delete user data.
3.  **Do not** publicly disclose the issue until we have had a reasonable time to address it.
4.  Submit your report to security@example.com (replace with actual email).

## What to Include

*   Description of the vulnerability
*   Steps to reproduce
*   Potential impact
*   Proof of concept (if available)

## Our Commitment

We will:

*   Acknowledge your report within 48 hours.
*   Keep you updated on our progress.
*   Credit you for your discovery (if you wish).

Thank you for helping us keep our software safe.
